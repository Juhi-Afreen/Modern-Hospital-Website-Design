You can securely access your medical records 24/7 through our Patient Portal app. Alternatively, you can request physical copies by visiting the Medical Records Department on the ground floor, Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Please bring a valid photo ID.

General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. For the Intensive Care Unit (ICU), visiting hours are limited to 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM. We limit visitors to two per patient at a time to ensure a restful environment for recovery.
